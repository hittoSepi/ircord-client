# Voice Chat

## Starting a Call
- `/call <user>` тАФ Call another user
- `/accept` тАФ Accept an incoming call

## During a Call
- `/mute` тАФ Toggle your microphone
- `/deafen` тАФ Mute all incoming audio
- `/ptt` тАФ Switch to push-to-talk mode
- **F1** тАФ Hold to talk (when PTT is active)
- `/hangup` тАФ End the call

## Audio Settings
Open `/settings` to configure audio input/output devices.
